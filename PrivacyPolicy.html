PRIVACY POLICY

Refunds and cancellations cannot be made regarding online bookings except in exceptional circumstances. Full payment is required for advanced bookings to secure your bowling lane.

The Purpose of our Privacy Policy

This policy has been developed because it is our desire that you feel safe and secure about your security and privacy when it comes to your personal information while using our webiste. Personal information can refer to your first and last name, a home and email adress and other types of contact information that is capable of identifying you as an individual.

Collecting Personal Information

By visiting our website we collect information from your computer automatically such as your IP address and browser type. Information is only used in anonymous form, instead of being linked to their respective individuals.

We also collect your personal information when you use features on our website such as booking or purchasing goods and anytime you correspond with us.

What We Do With It

The following list is a summary of how we would use your personal information:

  • - When you make an enquiry to us, we use your personal information to respond.
  • - To store infromation about you such as your preferences to help improve your online experience ad visits.
  • - When we have special offers and promotions we would like to share with you, we need your personal information where you have given us permission to contact you.
  • - Processing your online bookings and other transactions.
  • - To send you information we think that may be of interest to you such as bowling events, newsletters and other types of information.
