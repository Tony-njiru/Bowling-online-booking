TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Refunds and cancellations cannot be made regarding online bookings except in exceptional circumstances. Full payment is required for advanced bookings to secure your bowling lane.

Payment will be taken in the case we are unable to get a replacement reservation. Postponing your booking is the same as cancelling and you will be still be charged if we are unable to fill your reservation.

Bookings

Full payment is needed in advance for all online booking of booking. Pre-booking of bowling is available only online. All of our other activities on our website can be pre-booked through our call centre or by visiting Bowled Over directly.

Bowling Prices

Online Bookings can be made up to 4 months in advance.

Online bookings can be made for up to 20 people. For groups larger than 20 please contact our team of advisers on 0715/0780-557775.

Bookings for multiple games cannot be done in advance. Lanes may be booked only one game at a time.

Our call centre is open 7 days a week, between 8am - 10pm. Calls cost as per your phone company'sservice providers.

Cancellation and Refunds

Bowled Over offers a no refund policy on any and all bookings made directly in person, through our call centre or online.

Refunds will not be offered if you wish to cancel your booking.

Privacy and Protection

Any and all information provided from which your identity can be identified will only be used in accordance within the data protection laws.
