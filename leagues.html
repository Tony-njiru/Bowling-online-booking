KIDS BOWL FREE!

Although this year’s Kids Bowl Free program was off to a late start due to Covid, it is now up and running for you and your kids bowling enjoyment. Visit www.kidsbowlfree.com to sign up. Here are the times available for Kids Bowl Free that will be in effect until September 30th, 2021:

  • Monday ~ Closed
  • Tuesday ~ Closed
  • Wednesday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Thursday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Friday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Saturday ~ Not Available
  • Sunday ~ CLOSED
Lane availability is reduced due to the province’s social distancing mandate so we encourage you to call in advance for a reservation. Walk-ins will be accepted based on lane availability. Trent Valley Lanes is prepared to extend the Kids Bowl Free program beyond September 30th, 2021 if there are any delays in the start of the school year.
