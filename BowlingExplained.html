BOWLING EXPLAINED

To be a bowler, you must first learn the way of the bowler. Here are some terms that should help you understand bowling.

Strike

You get 10 points for knocking down 10 pins with one ball.

Spare

You get 10 points for knocking down 10 pins with two consecutive balls.

Foul Line

The line at the start of the lane.

Foul

Stepping over the line.

Gutter

The section on either side of the lane. This is where your ball goes coming off the lane or if you miss.
