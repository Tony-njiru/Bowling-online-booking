LASER COMBAT

Shoot for the stars

You have been chosen along with others to participate in a battle that will decide the fate of your home planet. The only way for you to survive is to obliterate the opposing forces. Go solo or form a team and save your home planet! Laser Combat will test all your skills in the field. Only the best of the best will survive in this epic experience!

Show your skills and try to survive in one of three battlefields, will you save everyone?

WHAT YOU NEED TO DO

Each game lasts for 40 mins with 10 minutes at the start to brief you on your mission and 30 mins in the field. There will also be support from a crew member so don't worry, they will help you with whatever you need.
